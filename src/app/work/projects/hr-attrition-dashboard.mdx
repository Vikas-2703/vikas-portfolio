## Overview
Developed a **4-page Power BI dashboard** to analyse employee attrition patterns, identify key drivers, and provide drill-down insights at employee level.

The solution enables HR teams to move from static reporting to **interactive, data-driven decision-making**.

## Business Problem
High employee attrition impacts organisational performance, but root causes are often unclear due to fragmented reporting and lack of drill-down capabilities.

## Solution
- Designed an interactive dashboard with KPI tracking and deep-dive analytics  
- Implemented **attrition rate calculations and workforce metrics using DAX**  
- Built **multi-page navigation with drill-through and decomposition tree analysis**  
- Enabled exploration across dimensions like department, job role, age, and satisfaction
